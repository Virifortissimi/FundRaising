Frequently Asked Questions

Is ScholarX App Free For Download?

Yes. For now it is only available for Android and iOS/iTunes.

What do I get after i download the App?

After Downloading on Google Play store for free, Users will have access to available general scholarships.

What is Village by ScholarX, and how can i benefit from it?

Village by ScholarX is an education crowdfunding platform where students (Secondary, Undergraduate and Graduate) seeking financial assistance can create a funding request to pitch themselves to the public on why they should receive donations to help fund their tuition fee or purchase items needed in School.

Why do i need to unlock more Scholarships and How much does it cost to unlock?

You need to unlock the hidden scholarships as they are not always made known to the general public also some are not always published yearly but with ScholarX, we make them available. ₦1000/Year. This is a one-time payment every year.

Is there a limit to the amount i can request for?

Yes. Secondary School: ₦20,000 - ₦50,000, Tertiary Institutions: ₦30,000 - ₦70,000. However, it is advisable to request for the exact amount you need for Tuition fee, Accommodation or other items needed in School i.e Computers, Textbooks and Notebooks e.t.c

Who can apply for funds?

Verified students in Secondary Schools and Tertiary Institution.

How can i submit an application for fund request that will be approved?

Create an account, Login, Upload a 200Kb or less picture with good quality,answer the relevant questions, write a compelling story on why you need the fund and state the amount you urgently need. An application with a Youtube Link gives additional edge

Is there any Admin charge that will be deducted after i eventually get sponsored?

Yes, We deduct 10% admin fee from the funds.

I am in a Private/Public Secondary School. How will the funds get to me?

Private Schools - fully funded request for Tuition fee will be paid directly to the private school in the name of the student. Public schools - fully funded requests will be used to get the items needed and it will be delivered directly to the student

Do i need a account before applying for funds?

Yes, You can create an account using a valid email address and a phone number.
